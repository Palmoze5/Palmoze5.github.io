About Me

I am a motivated and resourceful full-stack web developer with a passion for solving problems and helping people succeed. I have 10 years of experience working effectively in teams and engaging with stakeholders.


My work with nonprofits served as an unexpected introduction to web development and allowed me to dabble in a bit of coding. I asked myself, “Is there a way for me to go deeper and possibly work as a web developer?” This question led me to General Assembly (GA). Their Full-Stack Web Developer Immersive bootcamp was the fastest way for me to learn what web development entails and to change careers to the exciting developer world.


I am thrilled with the opportunity to use and improve upon the skills gained at GA by working for a mission-minded organization! Check out my work and contact me below.
